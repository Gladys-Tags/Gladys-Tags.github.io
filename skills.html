SKILLS

CONTEMPORARY

I use to dance since I was in primary school. Breaking the conventional style of dance, contemporary focuses on the freedom of movement. When performing, dancers focus on connecting their mind and body through fluid dance moves.

BALLROOM

I was able to dance ballroom when I was in my secondary days. Ballroom dancing will make you stronger, happier, healthier, and smarter. It will bring you joy. Dancing is an excellent cardiovascular exercise that will use all your major muscle groups. It encourages good posture and body alignment, and will strengthen your body's core abdominal muscles.

JAZZ
